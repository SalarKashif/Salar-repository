Celebrity Animals


Toshi :)

Toshi is a famous Black Rhino that is housed at the Miami Zoo. Toshi was born in Hiroshima Japan, in 1977, and then found a permanent home at Miami Zoo in 1983. This gentle giant is a hit with crowd and loves being the zoo's ambassador. From celebrities to school children, Toshi's charm takes everyone by suprise.

Joey :3

Joey was an orphaned baby sea otter discovered on the coast of Kyuquot, British Columbia, in July 2020. Joey was found crying next to his already dead mom, and was taken to Ocean Wise Marine Mammal Rescue Center where he recieved around-the-clock care. Joey was streamed to Youtube where his daily antics gained him a legion of fans. After 33 days, Joey was transferred to the Vancouver Aquarium, but his fame continues. There is a 24/7 cam over the otter lagoon where fans can watch him play with his newfound family.
